We hold regular meetups with talks on offensive security, reverse engineering, OSINT, privacy, hardware hacking, and more. All skill levels are welcome — from first-year students to seasoned professionals.

We believe in the hacker ethos: curiosity, creativity, and sharing knowledge freely. No gatekeeping. No drama. Just learning.

// Code of Conduct

⚠ DEFCON Standard COC

  • Be excellent to each other. No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination of any kind.
  • Respect the venue and other attendees. Leave it better than you found it.
  • No unauthorized testing of networks, devices, or systems — at events or otherwise.
  • Talks and demos should be educational, not malicious. Know the difference.
  • Violations may result in removal from events and the community. No exceptions.
  • When in doubt, ask an organizer. We're here to help.
